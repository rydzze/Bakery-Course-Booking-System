About 4PeopleBakery

Our History


4 People Bakery is a small, Malaysia-based bakery chain. As of February 2022, it operates three locations in Melaka, five in Kuala Lumpur, and six in Selangor. Its original bakery opened in 2002 in Taman prima saujana, kajang, selangor, Malaysia. 4 People Bakery was opened in 2002, on the site of two previous bakeries; its owners, James, had previously run the Bay Village Breads bakery in Point Reyes and Mill Valley. After the success of their San Francisco bakery they also opened a new bakery in Malaysia and published a book of recipes from their bakery. Recall where the company initially started, the bakery was organised by 4 people and they have a meeting before they operate the company business, what they come to a conclusion is that they nearly disagree with each other during the meeting but by the following month all of them take a concession and finally get into partnership. Initially, the company was named as 3 People Bakery. The name changed in April 1990 to 4 People Bakery after the joint force from James's friend with an investment check worth RM100,000. They had a hard time together after the business launched, they even don’t have enough money to rent an office so they work at a small dormitory. Although it is hard, they grit their teeth and keep on going no matter what the problem they’re going to face. What is fortunate is, the 4 People Bakery went through all of this with teamwork and belief among the company members.


Over 5 years of operating, 4 People Bakery has caught attention not only customers but also Company ABC which is a publicly listed company as well, Company ABC would like to make an investment with 4 People Bakery for a value of RM 300,000. With this investment, 4 People Bakery made an upgrade for they dormitory, they decides to make their dormitory to become a larger room rather than buying a office for them to work on as all of them unanimously agreed that they want to have a good memory regarding all the hardworks they had put in their bakery company. After a few years passes through and now was 2022 year, their bakery are now a famous marketer and they plan to offer some courses to promote their products and what's more important is they are getting older and older, they’re not willing to watch their recipe and all of these hardworks they get through before their bakery became a famous bakery shop to “extinct”.
